The Marmoset Coordinating Center (MCC) joins real-time census data on marmosets and expert marmoset neuroscientists, which together provide information and access to animals for scientists in the broader neuroscience community.

FACILITATING THE USE OF MARMOSETS IN NEUROSCIENCE RESEARCH

The goals of the MCC are to coordinate information sharing among marmoset colonies and to provide information and support to neurscience investigators who are interested in using marmosets in their research programs.
